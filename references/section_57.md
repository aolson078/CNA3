# [57.0] COMMONWEALTH

SUPPLY BASE
GENERAL RULE:
The Commonwealth Player has an unlimited 
amount of supplies of all types — fuel, ammuni-
tion, water, and stores — in Cairo at all times dur-
ing the game. His problem is solely to get it to 
where he wants it. He does not ship supplies in; if 
he wants something, it is in Cairo. Remember, 
Commonwealth ships need no supplies. For rein-
forcements and replacements for personnel and 
equipment, the Commonwealth Player should 
consult his Reinforcement Schedule and CW 
Replacement System (see Section 20.0)
